φ distribution

Use this to specify a φ distribution of the particle size. The value φ is the negative base 2 log of the particle diameter in millimeters. To specify a distribution of particles, use the format φ1=p1;φ2=p2;... where p is the percentage of particles with the corresponding value of φ. Use a semi-colon to separate each specification. For example, the value of 1=25.5;2=50;3=24.5 means that 25.5% of the particles have a diameter of 2^(-1)=0.5 mm, 50% have a diameter of 2^(-2)=0.25 mm, and the remaining 24.5% have 2^(-3)=0.125 mm. If the percentages do not add to 100, they will be normalized.

This option is exclusive of specifying a normal distribution.
