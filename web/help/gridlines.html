gridlines

This option puts gridlines on the map. Gridlines can vary
  • in frequency in both the latitude and longitude direction
  • in thickness (by pixels)
  • in color.
Some example specifications are
  • 5 - lines are drawn every 5 degrees in both the latitude and longitude direction.
  • 5:2 - Same as above and lines are 2-pixels wide.
  • 5:2blue - Same as above and lines are blue.
  • 5:2x10:3white - 2-pixel wide white lines are drawn every 5 degrees in the longitude direction and 3-pixel wide white lines are drawn every 10 degrees in the latitude direction.
